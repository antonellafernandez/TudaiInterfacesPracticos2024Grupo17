Frostfire Clash: Scorpion vs. Subzero

How to Play

  • 1. Before starting, choose your player skin to customize your pieces.
  • 2. Select the board size in the options. If you do not select a size, the game will start in classic mode (Connect 4).
  • 3. Press "Start Game" to begin
  • 4. Select your piece and drag it to the board to place it in the desired column.
  • 5. Connect 4/5/6/7 pieces (depending on the board) in any direction: vertical, horizontal, or diagonal before your opponent to win!

Challenge a friend in a strategic battle of wits inspired by the rivalry of Subzero and Scorpion! Engage in a thrilling game of Connect 4 as you drop your icy blue or fiery yellow discs into the grid. Aim to be the first to align 4 in a row while blocking your opponent's moves to prevent them from achieving victory. Will you outsmart your rival and claim the title? When the match concludes, simply pull out the slider bar to release all the discs and ignite the competition again!
